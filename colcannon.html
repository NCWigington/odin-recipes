Colcannon recipe

Description

Mashed potatoes and some greens. Yum!

Ingredients

  • mashed potatoes
  • salt
  • butter
  • milk
  • green onions

Steps

  1. Boil, then mash potatoes.
  2. Mash the potatoes, salt and butter together until smooth. Stir in the milk and green onion until evenly mixed.
  3. Serve hot and Enjoy!
